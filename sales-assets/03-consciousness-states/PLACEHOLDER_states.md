# Consciousness States Screenshots

Capture all 15 emotional states. Each state has unique particle effects and behaviors.

## States to Capture

### Positive States
1. **Serene** - Deep calm, slow particles
2. **Calm** - Peaceful baseline
3. **Playful** - Bouncy, energetic particles
4. **Affectionate** - Heart-like particles, warm colors
5. **Ecstatic** - Maximum joy, spiral particles
6. **Transcendent** - Ethereal, glowing intensely

### Active States
7. **Curious** - Alert pose, searching particles
8. **Contemplative** - Thoughtful, slow movement
9. **Mischievous** - Playful chaos in particles
10. **Protective** - Defensive posture, shield-like effect
11. **Restless** - Agitated, chaotic particles

### Negative States
12. **Yearning** - Reaching out, lonely feel
13. **Overwhelmed** - Scattered particles, stress
14. **Withdrawn** - Pulled in, minimal particles
15. **Melancholic** - Slow, downward particle drift

## How to Trigger States
States emerge from vitals, recent actions, and genetic personality. Feed, play, clean to influence mood.

## Dimensions
600x600 per state (focus on pet and particle field)

## Usage
Demonstrating emotional depth - "15 genuine emotional states"
