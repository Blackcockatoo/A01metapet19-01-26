## All 17 Achievements by Category

### Care (3)
1. **Novice Caretaker** (Bronze) - Keep vitals above 50 for 1 hour
2. **Master Caretaker** (Gold) - Keep vitals above 80 for 24 hours
3. **Perfect Day** (Platinum) - Maintain all vitals at 100 for 1 hour

### Battle (4)
4. **First Victory** (Bronze) - Win your first battle
5. **Battle Veteran** (Silver) - Win 25 battles
6. **Battle Master** (Gold) - Win 100 battles
7. **Unstoppable** (Platinum) - Win 10 battles in a row

### Exploration (4)
8. **First Scan** (Bronze) - Explore your first Vimana cell
9. **Field Researcher** (Silver) - Collect 50 field samples
10. **Anomaly Hunter** (Gold) - Resolve 10 anomalies
11. **Cartographer** (Platinum) - Explore all Vimana cells

### Evolution (3)
12. **First Evolution** (Bronze) - Evolve to NEURO stage
13. **Quantum Being** (Gold) - Reach QUANTUM stage
14. **Speciation** (Platinum) - Achieve final SPECIATION stage

### Social/Breeding (3)
15. **First Offspring** (Bronze) - Breed your first offspring
16. **Lineage Keeper** (Silver) - Create 5 offspring
17. **Dynasty Founder** (Gold) - Create 20 offspring

## Tier Points
- Bronze: 10 points
- Silver: 25 points
- Gold: 50 points
- Platinum: 100 points
