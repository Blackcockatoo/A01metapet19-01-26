## Main Screenshots Needed

### Grid Overview

- Mix of explored and unexplored cells
- Field type colors visible

### Field Types (4 types)
1. **Calm (Teal)** - Peaceful energy field
2. **Neuro (Purple)** - Mental/consciousness field
3. **Quantum (Amber)** - Quantum energy field
4. **Earth (Emerald)** - Grounded, natural field

### Exploration States
- **Unexplored Cell** - Dark/hidden
- **Explored Cell** - Revealed with intensity
- **Anomaly Detected** - Special indicator visible
- **Sample Collection** - Collecting samples animation

### Anomaly Types
- Energy anomaly (+15 energy)
- Mood anomaly (+12 mood)
- Rare anomaly (+20 both)
