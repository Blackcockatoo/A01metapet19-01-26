# Pet Variations Screenshots

Capture different pet body types and features. Each pet is genetically unique.

## Body Types to Capture
- **Spherical** - Round, cute form
- **Crystalline** - Angular, gem-like form
- **Pyramidal** - Mystical triangular form

## Features to Capture
- **Horns** - Crystal antlers
- **Wings** - Ethereal wing effects
- **Aura** - Glowing energy field
- **Crown** - Golden crown accessory
- **Third Eye** - Mystical forehead eye
- **Tail Flame** - Burning tail effect

## How to Get Variations
Generate multiple pets to see different genetic expressions. Each pet's genome determines its appearance.

## Dimensions
800x800 per variation (square crop around pet)

## Usage
Showcasing genetic uniqueness - "No two pets are alike"
