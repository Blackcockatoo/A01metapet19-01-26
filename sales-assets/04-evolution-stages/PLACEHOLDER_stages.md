# Evolution Stages Screenshots

Capture all 4 evolution stages showing progression.

## Stages to Capture

### 1. GENETICS (Starting Stage)
- Basic pet form
- Evolution panel showing "GENETICS" active
- Requirements for next stage visible

### 2. NEURO (First Evolution)
- Slightly enhanced form
- "NEURO" highlighted in evolution panel
- Celebration message if capturing the moment of evolution

### 3. QUANTUM (Advanced Stage)
- Noticeable visual enhancements
- Quantum effects visible
- Evolution panel showing advanced progress

### 4. SPECIATION (Final Form)
- Full evolved form
- Maximum visual complexity
- "SPECIATION" achieved indicator

### Evolution Transition (GIF)
- Capture the moment of evolution transition
- 5-10 second GIF showing the celebration animation

## Requirements to Reach Each Stage
- NEURO: 30min playtime, vitals avg >50
- QUANTUM: 1hr playtime, vitals avg >60
- SPECIATION: 4hr playtime, vitals avg >70

## Dimensions
1280x720 per stage, GIF at 800x600

## Usage
Demonstrating long-term progression and growth journey
